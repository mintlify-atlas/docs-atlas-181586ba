## Integration Types

Formbricks supports two types of integrations:

### Direct Integrations

Direct integrations (Slack, Notion, Google Sheets) use OAuth authentication and allow you to:
- Connect with a few clicks
- Select specific surveys to sync
- Map survey questions to destination fields
- Automatically send responses as they arrive

### Webhook-based Integrations

Webhook and API-based integrations (Zapier) provide:
- Flexible trigger configurations
- Custom data transformations
- Support for complex workflows
- Integration with thousands of apps

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    In your Formbricks dashboard, go to **Configuration** > **Integrations**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Integration">
    Click **Connect** on the integration you want to set up
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Follow the OAuth flow to grant Formbricks access to your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Sync">
    Select which surveys and questions to sync, then map them to your destination
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Data Gets Synced?

Depending on the integration, the following data can be synced:

- **Response data**: Answers to survey questions
- **Metadata**: Timestamp, user agent, country, device type
- **Contact attributes**: User identification data (if configured)
- **Survey information**: Survey name, type, and status
- **Custom variables**: Any variables you've set in your survey logic
